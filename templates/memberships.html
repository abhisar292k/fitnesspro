Common Questions

What's included in the fitness assessment?

Our comprehensive assessment includes body composition analysis, strength testing, flexibility evaluation, and personalized goal setting with a certified trainer.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, all members can freeze their membership up to 3 months per year. Elite members get unlimited freezing privileges.

How do I book personal training sessions?

Easily book your sessions through our mobile app or at the front desk. We recommend booking 24 hours in advance.
